Terms & Conditions
1. Registration and Admission

Admission to Aranshi Education Hub is subject to availability of seats. The management reserves the right to admit or refuse admission.

2. Fee Payment

Fees must be paid in full at the time of admission or as per the installment plan agreed upon. Late payments may incur additional charges.

3. Attendance

Regular attendance is mandatory. A minimum of 80% attendance is required to be eligible for tests and examinations.

4. Code of Conduct

Students are expected to maintain discipline and decorum. Any misconduct may lead to disciplinary action including termination of admission.

5. Refund Policy

Fee once paid is non-refundable except in cases where the institute is unable to provide the service.
